Member of Maharastra Medical Council.

Since 2010, Dr. Kasat has been a highly respected pulmonologist, known for his dedication and expertise in respiratory health. Specializing in a wide range of respiratory conditions including TB, ILD, allergy, asthma, COAD, sleep apnea, snoring, and lung cancer, Dr. Kasat provides precision care that focuses on each patient’s unique needs. His skills in advanced procedures such as bronchoscopy, thoracocentesis, and other critical respiratory interventions are matched by his compassionate approach to patient care.

As an Assistant Professor at a leading medical college, Dr. Kasat brings over two decades of teaching experience, mentoring both undergraduate and postgraduate students, and holds MMC accreditation. A frequent speaker at regional and state conferences, he is committed to advancing pulmonary health through continuous learning and shared knowledge. Dr. Kasat’s vision is simple yet profound: to help every patient breathe freely and achieve respiratory wellness through expert, personalized care.
